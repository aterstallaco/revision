Top Techniques

Explore these powerful techniques to enhance your creativity and problem-solving skills.

Cognitive Reframing

Change your perspective on a situation to find new possibilities.

Perspective Shifting

Walk in someone else's shoes to see a problem from a different angle.

Awe Spotting

Find inspiration in the vastness of nature and the universe.

Pattern Breaking

Break out of your routines to discover new ways of doing things.

Question Storming

Challenge assumptions by asking questions instead of seeking answers.

Sensory Immersion

Engage your senses to fully experience and absorb your surroundings.

Reverse Association

Flip your problem on its head and find a new way to approach it.
